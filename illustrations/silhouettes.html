Problem:

To understand the effects and interactions of positive and negative space.


Action:

I cut out silhouettes of a couple on black construction paper and pasted it on white Bristol. Then, I cut out the same silhouettes on white paper and pasted it on a black background to create a reflection of the couple.


Results:

A romantic and mysterious scene of a couple and their reflection in the water that shows how positive and negative space interact and create visually stunning effects.


Tools Used:

Colored paper
