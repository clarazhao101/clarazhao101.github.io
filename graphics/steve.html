Problem:

To create a poster for a leader in the arts and sciences that highlights their personality or work.


Action:

I chose Steve Jobs for my leader because I had always used and loved Apple products. The challenge with designing a Steve Jobs poster is that everyone has an idea of what a typical Apple ad looks like, but I wanted to steer away from making a commercial advertisement for an Apple product and focus more on how Steve Jobs and Apple impacted the world at large.


Results:

A poster that has Apple vibes but highlights how Steve Jobs and Apple helped connect all the different parts of the world.


Tools Used:

Adobe Photoshop
