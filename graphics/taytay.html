Problem:

To create a poster for Taylor Swift’s Reputation tour that captures the feeling of her music and personality.


Action:

I used the concept of a broken mirror because a mirror symbolizes someone's image and how they are perceived, and thus the cracks in the mirror represent the fragmentation of Taylor's image or reputation as she evolved as an artist and person. On some of the shards, I included traces of "old Taylor" with her golden curls and more innocent look to show that despite the fragmentation, the essence of what made Taylor Swift special in the first place is still there if you looked closely.


Results:

A visually intense and slightly haunting piece that reflects Taylor’s dilemma with her image and Reputation.
